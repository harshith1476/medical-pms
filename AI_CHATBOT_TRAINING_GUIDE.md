- Paracetamol 500mg every 6-8 hours
- Cool compress application
- Monitor temperature regularly

- Warm fluids and steam inhalation
- Rest and vitamin C

- Cold/warm compress
- Hydration

- Honey and warm water
- Steam inhalation
- Throat lozenges
- Avoid irritants
